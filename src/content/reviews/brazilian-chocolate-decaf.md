If you asked me to recommend a decaf for someone new to home espresso, this would probably be it. It doesn't ask anything unusual of you.

## As espresso and milk

The shot pulled dark and syrupy, with a nice thick crema. Steamed into oat milk it stayed present all the way through — chocolate on top, chocolate at the bottom of the cup.

## The one downside

If you're the sort of person who likes to sniff and slurp and pick out three separate notes, this coffee will feel a little one-note. That's fine by me at 4pm on a Sunday.
