I made about fifteen brews from this bag: mostly V60, one messy AeroPress inversion experiment, and a couple of espressos out of curiosity. The filter brews were the standouts.

## On filter

At around 15g coffee to 250g water, medium grind, it was juicy and clean. The apple note came through nicely without any of the "sharp" edges that put people off lighter roasts.

## Would I re-order?

Yes. Not as my only decaf — I'd want something chocolatey alongside it — but as a genuinely nice filter option this is very easy to like.
