I ran through this bag first as espresso, then again for a few days as a flat white using barista oat milk. It stayed friendly the whole way through — no funny surprises, no sudden dip in flavour once it opened up. If you like a mug of coffee that tastes like coffee, this is a safe recommendation.

## Dialling in on espresso

I ended up at roughly 18g in, 40g out over 30 seconds at 93°C. Anything hotter and the bitterness pushed forward; anything cooler and it turned a bit flat. Once dialled in it was very repeatable — which matters if you're half asleep at 6am.

## What surprised me

I usually find sugar cane decafs a touch soft on the finish. This one had more sweetness lingering than I expected — that caramel note held on for a good few seconds.
