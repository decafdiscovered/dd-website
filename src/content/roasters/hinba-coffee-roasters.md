Hinba started on a small Hebridean island in 2019 and now roast every Monday out of Glasgow, shipping single origins within a week of roasting. Their decaf slot is treated the same as any other seasonal lot вЂ” right now it's La Mariposa from Colombia вЂ” which is a nice change from decaf being an afterthought on the menu.
