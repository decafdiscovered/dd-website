Carter Coffee roast in Edinburgh and keep things fresh with a weekly Wednesday and Friday roast schedule. Alongside blends like Bread & Butter and Choccy Block they run a rotating cast of single origins, and their El Buho sugarcane-process Colombian decaf is worth a look for anyone chasing a chocolatey, well-behaved decaf.
